The tissue is modelled with a surface blood vessel (1mm diameter), and pathlength has been calculated with MC simulations for 181 detectors.

Pathlength is contained in a matrix of shape (181x601), with 181 detectors and 601 wavelengths.

The last detector is located on the blood vessel, then each detector are spaced 100µm to each other, so that the first detetor is located on the grey matter at 1.8 cm from the blood vessel (see Fig. 4 https://www.spiedigitallibrary.org/journals/neurophotonics/volume-6/issue-04/045015/Intraoperative-quantitative-functional-brain-mapping-using-an-RGB-camera/10.1117/1.NPh.6.4.045015.full)
